## 1. Purpose

[COMPANY NAME] depends on the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of customer and company information. This policy sets the high-level security expectations all workforce members must follow. Specific procedures are documented in the supporting policies referenced in §8.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All workforce members (full-time, part-time, contractor, intern).
- All systems used to access, store, process, or transmit company or customer data — including production infrastructure, source code, ticketing systems, and corporate email.
- All locations from which company work is performed.

## 3. Roles and responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| **[CEO/CISO]** | Approves this policy; ultimate accountability for security posture. |
| **Security Officer** | Maintains policies; coordinates audit response; tracks the risk register. |
| **Engineering Manager** | Owns implementation of technical controls; quarterly access reviews. |
| **People Operations** | Onboarding/offboarding; security training compliance. |
| **All workforce members** | Follow policies; report incidents promptly; complete required training. |

## 4. Security objectives

[COMPANY NAME] aims to:
- Protect customer data from unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure.
- Maintain availability of customer-facing services consistent with published SLAs.
- Comply with applicable laws and contractual commitments (GDPR, CCPA, customer DPAs).
- Continuously improve our security posture through annual risk assessment and quarterly control reviews.

## 5. Acceptable use

Workforce members must follow the [Acceptable Use Policy](08-acceptable-use.md). Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

## 6. Risk management

A risk assessment is performed at least annually and reviewed quarterly. The risk register is maintained by the Security Officer. See [Risk Management Policy](09-risk-management.md).

## 7. Training

All workforce members must complete security awareness training within 30 days of joining and annually thereafter. Engineering staff additionally complete secure-development training.

## 9. Enforcement

Violations are addressed by the Security Officer in coordination with People Operations and the workforce member's manager. Repeated or serious violations may result in termination of employment or contract.

## 10. Exceptions

Exceptions to this policy must be documented and approved by the Security Officer. Exceptions are reviewed at least annually.

## 11. Review

This policy is reviewed annually by the Security Officer and re-approved by the [CEO/CISO]. Material changes require re-approval out of cycle.
