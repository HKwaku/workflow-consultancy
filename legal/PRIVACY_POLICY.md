## 4. AI processing

User-generated content (chat messages, process descriptions, document content) is sent to AI providers (Anthropic Claude; optionally Voyage AI for embeddings; optionally OpenAI when enabled) to generate responses, recommendations, and findings.

**Anthropic and Voyage do not train on customer API traffic.** OpenAI's policy varies by tier; consult their terms.

When you set a customer-managed API key (BYO), the corresponding LLM calls bill to **your** provider account, not ours. We still process the request to assemble the prompt, but the AI vendor's terms govern that traffic separately.

## 5. International transfers

Data may be processed in jurisdictions outside your own. Specifically:
- **Supabase**: hosted in your selected region (US, EU, etc.)
- **Vercel**: edge network, primarily US
- **Anthropic, Voyage, OpenAI**: primarily US

For transfers from the UK / EEA to non-adequacy countries, we rely on **Standard Contractual Clauses** with each sub-processor.

## 6. Retention

| Data type | Retention |
|-----------|-----------|
| Account data | While account is active + 30 days after deletion (grace period) |
| Customer-uploaded content | Same — controlled by you, deletable any time |
| Audit logs | 2 years |
| Token usage ledger | 13 months (for monthly billing reconciliation) |
| Backups | 7-30 days depending on Supabase tier |
| Anonymised analytics | Indefinite |

After 30 days following account deletion, your data is anonymised: identifying fields are scrubbed, content is retained or deleted per your settings. See [`expunge-deleted-accounts`](../DIAGNOSTICS_CAPABILITIES.md#gdpr) for the technical procedure.
